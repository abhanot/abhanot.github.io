About Us

OUR STORY

Across our first year courses, we noticed that technology was at the center of all innovation and that the most compelling ideas cut across many disciplines. However, we wanted to understand the broader application of what we were learning. When talking about this with friends, we realized that many student clubs in the United States produced technology publications that helped students gain unique insights. Looking at Westernâ€™s USC Club Directory, we found there were no such similar clubs, so last summer, we began working on creating a new club at Western that would bring together students from a variety of academic backgrounds. After many hours of planning, we created the Western University Technology Review (WUTR). Our vision for WUTR is to be a monthly publication highlighting how technology is shaping every aspect of human life across five disciplines: Biomedicine, Artificial Intelligence/ Robotics, Business, Interaction, and Sustainability. WUTR is committed to transforming complicated concepts into more accessible content that helps readers navigate and gain a better understanding of our constantly evolving, technologically diverse world. In the development of this new club, we collaborated with Western academia, including professors from the Ivey School of Business, to gain important insights. We strongly believe that this ongoing collaboration will help us better understand how emerging technologies will impact the future.
