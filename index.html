INTRODUCTION

The Western University Technology Review (WUTR) is a student-run organization that arose as the result of cooperation and careful compilation of innovation across multiple facets. Modelled and managed by undergraduate students at the University of Western Ontario, we have embarked on a mission to illuminate how technology is shaping every aspect of human life – from Biomedicine and Artificial Intelligence, to Business Influences, Interaction and Sustainability. In a search for discovery and understanding, the Club aims to promote diversity, and to shape the minds of the future generation’s leaders through the uniting of various disciplines. Our editors have come together from all faculties to form an interdisciplinary mosaic of cognoscenti, in an effort to educate and aid readers in navigating a constantly evolving, technologically diverse world. The Western University Technology Review’s quarterly publications serve to predict the applications of the raw technologies that will impact tomorrow’s world, and to catalyze innovative thinking within our academic society.
